Proprietary Frameworks

The tools inside
Constitutional Intelligence

These frameworks were developed from real Canadian government experience and formalized in Constitutional Intelligence (FriesenPress, 2025). They are available to organizations through advisory engagements and the book itself.

IMPACT™

Information Architecture

The structural backbone of the book. IMPACT™ provides a systematic way to map, assess, and govern AI initiatives across the full institutional lifecycle — from inception to delivery to audit.

Constitutional Evaluation

A decision methodology for evaluating AI choices against constitutional responsibilities. Designed specifically for Westminster systems where legal and democratic accountability are non-negotiable.

Coordination Architecture

Five structured layers for coordinating AI governance across departments, mandates, and electoral cycles — preventing the siloed failures that cause Phoenix-style disasters.
