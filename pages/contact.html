Frequently Asked

Common questions

How do I get an autographed copy?

Order directly through fedethics.ca/books. Every print order placed through this site is personally autographed by Celio before shipping — at no extra charge.

How does the free consultation offer work?

Purchase 5 or more copies of either book through this site, then complete the claim form on the books page. The offer is redeemable as a virtual call or a live speaking engagement.

Can Celio speak at our event?

Yes. Celio accepts speaking engagements internationally. Use the form on this page or email celio.oliveira@fedethics.ca with your event date, location, and topic focus.

Can we order on invoice for our institution?

Yes. Institutional orders with formal invoicing are available. Email communications@fedethics.ca with your quantity, delivery address, and billing information.

Is Celio available for media and podcast appearances?

Yes. Celio is available for interviews on AI governance, ethical leadership, data strategy, and both books. Select "Media / Press Interview" or "Podcast Appearance" in the enquiry form.

Comment commander Étoile de Tournesol pour ma classe ou club de lecture?

Commandez directement via la page du livre. Pour 5 exemplaires ou plus, vous débloquez une rencontre avec l'auteur et une dédicace personnalisée pour chaque lecteur.

What is FedEthics Inc.?

FedEthics Inc. is Celio Oliveira's independent advisory and publishing company. All work published and all advisory services are delivered in a strictly personal capacity, independent of any government institution or employer.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We respond within 48 business hours. For time-sensitive requests, email celio.oliveira@fedethics.ca directly with "URGENT" in the subject line.
