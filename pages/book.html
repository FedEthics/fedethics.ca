About the Author

Celio Oliveira

Fellow — Future Government Institute (2026) · Global Top 100 AI Innovators (2025)
Chair, CDAO Canada Public Sector · PhD Candidate, University of Toronto

Celio Oliveira is the founder of FedEthics Inc. and one of Canada's leading voices on ethical AI governance. His work bridges data strategy, responsible AI, and institutional modernization — offering practical frameworks built from over 15 years of senior leadership experience in high-accountability environments.

He is the Chair of the CDAO Canada Public Sector, a Fellow at the Future Government Institute, a Visiting Faculty Member at the Canada School of Public Service, an Executive Advisor at the Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada, a finalist of the Public Sector Data Challenge 2024, and Finalist at the North American Software Testing and QE Awards 2019. He writes, advises, and publishes in a strictly personal capacity through FedEthics Inc., independent of any employer.

After witnessing skilled leaders struggle without proper tools, I was inspired to write these books — to offer practical blueprints for governance that strengthens performance while protecting civic legitimacy, and stories that remind us why it matters.
