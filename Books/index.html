Founder & CDO, FedEthics Inc.  ·  PhD Candidate, University of Toronto  ·  Global Top 100 Innovators in Data, Analytics & AI (2025)

Celio Oliveira is the only Canadian public servant recognized on the 2025 Global Top 100 Innovators in Data, Analytics and AI list. A visionary executive leader, he has led enterprise-wide AI and data transformation across the Government of Canada, serving as Chief Data Officer and senior advisor across 7+ federal departments including Finance Canada, PSPC, CBSA, Health Canada, Statistics Canada, Privy Council Office, and the National Research Council.

A PhD candidate at the University of Toronto's Faculty of Information — researching Just and Sustainable AI Governance in Parliamentary Democracies — Celio brings a rare combination of technical depth, institutional authority, and genuine human curiosity. He is a visiting faculty member at the Canada School of Public Service, a Fellow at the Future Government Institute, and sits on the Executive Editorial Board of CDO Magazine.

As founder of FedEthics Inc., he leads a platform dedicated to responsible AI governance at the intersection of public policy, institutional accountability, and ethical technology adoption. His work is guided by a single conviction: that a more just world is built one decision — and one story — at a time.

About This Book

Digital Democracy is the definitive executive guide for leaders navigating the governance of artificial intelligence in parliamentary democracies. It presents a principled, actionable architecture for responsible AI adoption — grounded in 15+ years of federal practice, academic rigour, and the ethical traditions of Canada's public sector.

This book is not a technology manual. It is a governance manifesto — for Deputy Ministers, Chief Data Officers, legislators, and institutional leaders who understand that how we adopt AI is as important as whether we adopt it at all.

About This Book

"Like a sunflower, she does not chase the light in desperation — she turns toward it with the quiet certainty that it will come."

Étoile de Tournesol — Sunflower Star — is a luminous work of literary fiction and personal reflection. A story of migration, belonging, love, and the quiet dignity of becoming. Drawing from the richness of multiple cultures and languages, it speaks to anyone who has ever searched for home in an unfamiliar sky, and found it — not in a place, but in the decision to keep turning toward the light.

Published under FedEthics Creative™ — the literary imprint of FedEthics Inc. — this work affirms that the same mind that architects governance frameworks can also hold tenderness, grief, and wonder.
