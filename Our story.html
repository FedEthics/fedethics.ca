About FedEthics

Built from the inside.
Shared in a personal capacity.

FedEthics Inc. was founded to close the gap between the complexity of AI governance and the practical tools that leaders actually need. Everything here is written, advised, and published independently — not on behalf of any employer.

Celio Oliveira
The person behind FedEthics
Founder · Author · AI Governance Advisor

Celio Oliveira has spent more than 15 years at the intersection of data, technology, and public administration — building decision-ready capabilities in environments where the stakes are high and the accountability is real. FedEthics Inc. is the vehicle through which he shares that experience, in a completely personal capacity, with leaders and readers who need it most.

Recognized as one of the Global Top 100 Leaders in Data, Analytics & AI in 2025 and a Fellow at the Future Government Institute in 2026, Celio is also Chair of the CDAO Canada Public Sector and a Visiting Faculty Member at the Canada School of Public Service — where he supports senior leaders on responsible AI implementation.

He earned his MSc in Human-Centred Data Science and his MBA in Digital Media, and is currently a PhD Candidate at the University of Toronto. He speaks English, French, Portuguese, and Spanish — and uses all four in his work.

After witnessing skilled leaders struggle without proper tools, I was inspired to write — to offer practical blueprints for governance that strengthens performance while protecting civic legitimacy.

What FedEthics Stands For

Principles that guide every engagement

FedEthics operates under a global Code of Conduct. These are not marketing statements — they are operational commitments.

01
Ethical First

Every engagement, framework, and publication is anchored in ethical responsibility. Not as a constraint, but as the foundation for lasting trust.

02
Practically Grounded

Governance frameworks are only valuable if they can be implemented. FedEthics bridges policy ambition with operational reality — every time.

03
Independently Expressed

All books, frameworks, and advisory work are published in a personal capacity. FedEthics speaks freely, credibly, and without institutional constraint.
