🏛  Who We Are

Bridging Policy & Practice

FedEthics is a consulting and advisory firm focused on Ethical AI, data governance, and data science delivery — helping organizations build decision systems that are trustworthy, secure, and implementable.

Our work bridges policy and practice: from governance operating models and interoperability playbooks to responsible automation, analytics productionization, and measurable adoption.

FedEthics is led by a Canadian data leader recognized in the global Data and AI communities. Our founder was recognized as one of the Global Top 100 Innovators in Data, Analytics, and AI in 2025, a Fellow of the Future Government Institute, and is actively engaged in international networks.

How We Deliver Value

Our engagements are built around a proven set of methodologies — each designed to make complex AI and data challenges tractable, auditable, and adoptable.

01

Ethical AI & Responsible AI Workshops — Impact assessment, transparency controls, monitoring frameworks, and governance accountability embedded from day one.

02

Data Governance Strategy — Frameworks, stewardship, standards, data quality scorecards, lineage, and communities of practice aligned to your operating model.

03

Data Literacy & Digital Transformation Blueprints — Capability-building pathways that evolve organizations from data-aware to data-driven.

04
