The Person Behind FedEthics

Celio Oliveira

Founder · Author · AI Governance Advisor

With over 15 years navigating the intersection of public service, data strategy, and emerging technology, Celio Oliveira is one of Canada's most recognised voices on responsible AI governance. A practitioner first, he brings frameworks forged in real institutions — not consulting decks.

Named a Global Top 100 AI Innovator (2025) and a Fellow at the Future Government Institute, Celio has led AI teams, authored published frameworks, and appeared as an international keynote speaker. His work bridges the gap between policy ambition and operational reality.
